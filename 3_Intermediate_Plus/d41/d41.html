The Best Movies According to Dilshad

My top 3 movies of all time

Extreme Job (2019)

To expose an organised crime group, a team of narcotics detectives goes undercover at a chicken restaurant.
However, things change when one of their recipes becomes famous unexpectedly.

Release date: 23 January 2019 (South Korea)
Director: Lee Byeong-heon
Running time: 1h 51m
Language: Korean


Kung Fu Hustle (2004)

An aspiring gangster pretends to be part of a notorious gang and tries to terrorise a neighbourhood..
However, unknown to him, three martial arts experts live there and are ready to take him down.

Release date: 23 December 2004 (Hong Kong)
Director: Stephen Chow
Running time: 1h 38m
Language: Thai, Cantonese, Mandarin


Fight Club (1999)

Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless soap salesman.
Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.

Release date: 15 October 1999 (USA)
Director: David Fincher
Running time: 2h 19m
Language: English
